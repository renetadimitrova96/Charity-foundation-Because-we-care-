Donate blood - give life!

At the beginning of 2018, the Foundation "Because We Care" launched its new initiative "Donate Blood - Giving Life!". The idea for this activity is by Zaria Dimitrova - a new member of the foundation from the end of 2017. Her friends have suffered a catastrophe and they needed urgent blood donation. She was deeply worried about them and hopeless of what the doctors said it was not known when a blood donor would be found.Then we unanimously decided that we would try to help both her friends and other people in the same situation. So far since the beginning of March, we have helped more than 50 people and continue. If you want to help, fill in the form below and within 7 days we will contact you! Thank you!
