Charity Foundation "Because We Care" was formed in 2011. Its founder is Reneta Dimitrova - student and volunteer in various initiatives on the territory of Varna. The foundation was created with the aim of creating every month a rally event in which every citizen can join with both financial and any other help - developing and distributing promotional materials for events, raising funds for a cause, etc. After eight years of formation of the foundation the team and consists of 200 volunteers. Our mission is to help as many people as we need each day!

Our partners are:
  • Bulgarian Red Cross Varna
  • Directorate "Preventions" of the Municipality of Varna
  • University of Economics - Varna
